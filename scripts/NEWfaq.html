Frequently Asked Questions

Q: WHERE CAN I BUY SCHOOL UNIFORM? +
A: You can buy school uniform from the school. Please visit the school for information on uniform and prices.
Q: IS THERE TRANSPORTATION PROVIDED BY THE SCHOOL OR DO LEARNERS NEED TO PROVIDE THEIR OWN? +
A: Sorry, the school does not provide transportation as yet, but we are working towards buying a bus or taxis for learners in far distances.
Q: Can I take my child on holiday during term time? +
A: No , Attending school is a compulsory obligation that parents must adhere to and therefore you should avoid taking your child out of school.
Q: What do I do if I need to see a teacher? +
A: In order to see a teacher you must set up an appointment first by email the admin on the email address found on our website .
Q: How do you communicate with parents? +
A: WE communicate with parents via the contact details provided on the registration forms . we also try to reach and interact with a larger crowd on our social media platforms.
